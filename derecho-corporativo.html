Corporate law

At B&CC, our group of experts have extensive experience in advising companies, both national and international, in the constitution, dissolution or liquidation of companies and ongoing advice during the lifecycle of all types of commercial companies, including amendments to the Bylaws, capital increases and reductions, termination and appointment of legal representatives, granting and revoking of powers, among others.

Likewise, our corporate law department can assist you in the drafting and negotiation of all types of commercial contracts and in the issuance of legal concepts on the scope and interpretation of contract clauses in which there are differences of interpretation.
